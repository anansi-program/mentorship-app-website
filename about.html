About Ananse

Welcome to Ananse! We're a group of alumni from the computer and biomedical engineering department at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology in Ghana. When we graduated and started our careers, we realized that there were a ton of opportunities we didn't know about. We wished we had someone to guide us and show us the ropes.

That's why we started the mentorship program. We pair up students (mentees) with experienced professionals (mentors) who can offer guidance, share their experiences, and help students achieve their career goals. So far, we've helped many students take the next step in their careers, and we're excited to keep growing and expanding.

Our goal is to make the transition from education to employment as smooth as possible for everyone. We believe that mentorship is the key to unlocking your potential and achieving your dreams. Join us today and let's take the next step together!
